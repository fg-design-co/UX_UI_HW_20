Bulls Headed for Best Season in Years

A combination of great acquisitions in the off-season, an All-Star roster, and high-caliber coaching

Alex Caruso (left), DeMar DeRozan (center), Lonzo Ball (right)

The Bulls acquired DeMar DeRozan, Lonzo Ball, and Alex Caruso in the off-season. The three players have taken the Bulls to the next level, with the Bulls standing at the top of the Eastern Conference. These signings have added scoring ability, passing acumen, and bench depth to the team, and their additions are reaping benefits for the Bulls.

General Management and Coaching Gives Franchise Direction

New hires in the Front Office and Coaching Staff have revitalizaed the much maligned franchise

Billy Donovan

The Bulls' turnaround in fortunes began with the hiring of Vice President of Basketball Operations Arturas Karnisovas and General Manager Marc Eversley. Their first major staff addition to the team was Head Coach Billy Donovan. His coaching has made the team greater than the sum of its parts, and given Chicago Bulls fans hope that the team is headed in the right direction.
